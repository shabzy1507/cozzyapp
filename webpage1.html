WHAT IS COZZY AND WHAT IT AIM TO ACHIEVE?

Cozzy app is a real-estate investment management application that will allow users, buyers, sellers and agents to access and manage all aspects of real-estate, including investing, buying, selling, renting, physical inspecting and security, reporting and analytics, and with safe transaction in view. Cozzy app can serve an assistant to help user never forget any little or important real-estates investement information in their lives.
